Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • White vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Black peppercorns
  • Bay leaves
  • Brown sugar
  • Chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Aside from the chicken thighs, mix all ingredients together to create the marinade
  2. Marinate the chicken thighs in the adobo sauce for at least an hour. To absorb the most flavor, you can marinate the chicken for as long as 3 hours. If you marinade the chicken too long, the texture of the chicken will be affected.
  3. Fry the chicken thighs until they are browned. They don't need to be cooked through, but try and focus on getting a nice golden-brown color and crispy texture.
  4. Once the chicken is browned and crispy, pour in the marinade and bring the chicken and sauce to a boil. Simmer until the sauce has reduced by half and the chicken is completely cooked through.
