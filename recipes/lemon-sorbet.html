Lemon Sorbet

Finished Product!

This recipe was created by my wonderful girlfriend who is an amazing cook! This sorbet is perfect for a hot day!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 3/4 cup water
  • 1 3/4 cups of sugar
  • 1.5 oz tequila

Steps

  1. Combine juice, zest, water, sugar and 1 oz tequila in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a boil and then turn off heat. Add remaining 0.5 oz tequila.
  2. Transfer liquid to the freezer and allow to cool 1-2 hours or until very cold but not frozen.
  3. Freeze in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions.
  4. Transfer back to freezer and allow to freeze for at least 1 more hour or until firm.
