Lasagna

This is a lasagna recipe that I love to cook for my family on special occassions! Making lasagna takes time with all it's layers and it is truly a labor of love.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Mozarrella cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Egg
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Parsely
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  • First, we must prepare the meat sauce.
    1. Brown the ground beef and sausage over medium-high heat.
    2. Drain excess fat.
    3. Add onion and garlic and cook and until softened.
    4. Stir in Marinara sauce, basil, and oregano.
    5. Simmer on low for at least 20 minutes.
  • Mix the Ricotta
    • In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, parmesan, parsley, and nutmeg.
  • Assemble the Layers
    • In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on thhe bottom in the following pattern:
      1. Noodles
      2. Ricotta mixture
      3. Mozzarellla cheese
      4. Meat sauce
    • Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, ending withh a thick layer of mozzarella on top.
  • Bake!
    • Preheat oven to 375°F
    • Bake for 25 minutes with the dish covered in foil
    • Remove the foil and bake for anothher 20 minutes until thhe cheese is bubbly and golden brown
    • Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before cutting
