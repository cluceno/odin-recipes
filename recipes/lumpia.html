Lumpia

Lumpia is a filipino version of an egg roll and classicly served with vinegar dipping sauce. The recipe is quite simple and enjoyment will likely depend on execution of the recipe. Good luck!

Ingredients

  • Green beans
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth cube
  • Ground black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Egg roll wrap
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Dice all vegetables into small strips.
  2. Sauté all vegetables in olive oil.
  3. Add chicken broth cube, slat, and ground black pepper for seasoning.
  4. Put vegetable mixture into egg roll wrap.
  5. Fry on medium-high heat until golden brown. Flip occassionally to ensure even cooking.
